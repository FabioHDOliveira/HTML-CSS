Welcome to DRG Karate Studio, founded in 1998 by Don Gosselin. We teach the American kenpo style of karate, a martial arts system created by the late Senior Grand Master Edmund Parker. Our students range from age 3 to 93. The goal of our studio is to teach martial arts in a fun, positive atmosphere while promoting physical fitness and a positive outlook on life.
